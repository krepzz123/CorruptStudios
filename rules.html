Community Rules

General Rules

  • 1.0 Trolling – Trolling is not tolerated.
  • 1.1 Metagaming – Using out of character information to gain in-game knowledge is not permitted.
    • Stream sniping.
    • Using OOC chat for in-game advantages.
    • Out-of-game communications (e.g. Discord/TeamSpeak) does not come under metagaming.
  • 1.2 Power Gaming – Using unrealistic in-game mechanics to alter a situation to your advantage.
    • /me ties your hands and other unrealistic /me statements.
    • Changing or removing clothing whilst under the initiation timer.
    • Changing vehicle livery during an active situation.
  • 1.3 Fail RP – Unrealistic behavior.
    • Repairing your vehicle in an active chase.
    • Taking a vehicle out of the garage whilst in an active chase.
    • Initiating on an officer during a traffic stop, this includes border force locations.
  • 1.4 RDM – Random deathmatch is the act of killing or purposefully damaging another player without valid initiation.
    • Initiation lasts 10 minutes.
    • To have valid initiation, you must meet the following criteria:
      • Be visible to the player.
      • Have a visible weapon withdrawn in your hands.
      • Give a clear verbal demand whilst specifying who you are initiating on, either by pointing your weapon or describing the player's clothing.
    • More details on initiation and valid kills are outlined here.
  • 1.5 No Reason to Initiate (NRTI) – You are not allowed to walk up to a player and initiate unless you have a valid reason.
    • Committing an illegal activity is a valid reason.
    • Seeing another player open their vehicle boot or inventory is not a valid reason to initiate.
    • Initiating at a traffic stop or border force location is not allowed.
  • 1.6 VDM – You may not use your vehicle as a weapon. If you have initiation on a person within a vehicle, you may attempt to disable their vehicle by ramming.
  • 1.7 Offensive Language/Toxicity – Antagonizing someone based on their race, gender, sexual orientation, age, disability, or religion/belief is unacceptable.
  • 1.8 Breaking Character – When in-game, you are not allowed to speak out of character unless an on-duty staff member is present or in a non-RP zone.
  • 1.9 Combat Logging – You are not allowed to leave the server whilst in an active situation or initiation.
  • 1.10 Combat Storing – You are not allowed to store items/weapons during an active situation to avoid being robbed/arrested.
  • 1.11 Exploiting – Abusing a bug or in-game mechanic to gain an advantage is not allowed.
  • 1.12 Out of Game Transactions (OOGT) – Selling or buying in-game assets for real-world money is not allowed.
  • 1.13 Spite Reporting – All valid player reports must be made within 24 hours. Reporting after this period will be considered spite reporting.
  • 1.14 Scamming – Scamming is not allowed under any circumstances.
  • 1.15 Loans – Loans are not permitted in the community.
  • 1.16 Wasting Admin Time – Wasting staff time and making situations unnecessarily prolonged is not allowed.

In Character Rules

  • 2.1 Value of Life – You must value your life at all times.
    • If you have no weapon out and someone points a gun at you, you must not take your weapon out.
    • If another player stands behind you with a weapon pointed at your head, you may not attempt to turn around and shoot them.
  • 2.2 Sexual RP – Sexual assault (non-consensual touching, rape, etc.) is not allowed.
  • 2.3 Terrorist RP – Terrorist roleplay is not allowed under any circumstance.
  • 2.4 Impersonation of Whitelisted Factions – Impersonating whitelisted factions such as Police or NHS is forbidden.
  • 2.5 GTA Online Driving – Actions such as flying helicopters too low in the city and performing unrealistic jumps are not allowed.
  • 2.6 New Life Rule (NLR) – After respawning, you cannot return to the area where you died for 15 minutes or remember anything from your previous life.
  • 2.7 Bad RP – Players will be banned for treating the server like GTA Online and performing actions like counting down during initiation or trash talking after a kill.
  • 2.8 Kidnapping – Hostage situations may only last a maximum of 30 minutes.

Faction Rules

  • 3.0 Theft of Emergency Vehicles – Stealing emergency service vehicles is allowed only with a valid RP reason.
  • 3.1 Whitelist Abuse – Misusing a faction whitelist is strictly prohibited.
  • 3.2 Cop Baiting – Actions intended to make the police engage with you or chase you, especially to start a firefight, are not allowed.
  • 3.3 Faction Kidnapping – Certain faction members such as PCSO’s and Custody Sergeant's cannot be taken hostage.
  • 3.4 Unrealistic Revival – Once revived, characters must behave naturally and not act as if they are unaffected by the situation.

Conclusion

These rules are put in place to ensure a fun, fair, and realistic role-playing experience for all players. Breaking these rules will result in consequences such as warnings, kicks, or bans depending on the severity of the offense. Please read and follow the rules to avoid unnecessary issues and contribute to a positive and immersive experience for everyone!
