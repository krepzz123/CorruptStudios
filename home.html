Welcome to Corrupt Public

CorruptStudios offers an immersive FiveM experience featuring a custom-built framework, law enforcement integration, and dynamic city systems. Whether you’re grinding, protecting, or scheming — there’s a place for you.

How to Play

Step 1: Download FiveM — First, make sure you have FiveM installed on your PC. You can get it from the official website at FiveM.net.

Step 2: Join Corrupt Public Server — Once FiveM is installed, join our server by entering the server IP or using our server listing.

FAQ

  • Q: Do I need to have a paid copy of Grand Theft Auto V to play?
  • A: Yes, you need a legitimate copy of GTA V to play on FiveM.
  • Q: Can I play on mobile?
  • A: Unfortunately, FiveM is only available for PC at this time.
  • Q: Is there a way to donate to support the server?
  • A: Yes! You can donate via our website. Donators get special perks to enhance the gameplay experience.
  • Q: How can I contact the admins?
  • A: You can contact the staff directly on our Discord server. Link available in the footer.
